Hello, everybody, I am the author of “A Dragon Idol’s Reincarnation Tale” and after 3 years of writing and posting chapters on Royal Roads, I am happy to say that the story will finally be released on Amazon and as an audiobook on Audible! 

[A Dragon Idol’s Reincarnation Tale Book 1: Awakening on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Awakening-LitRPG-Adventure-Dragon-Reincarnation-ebook/dp/B0C3NKVKWW)

[A Dragon Idol’s Reincarnation Tale Book 1: Awakening on Audible](https://www.audible.com/series/Dragon-Idols-Reincarnation-Tale-Audiobooks/B0C42XDDJ1)

It is an Isekai LitRPG that follows a girl reincarnated into the body of a dragon hatchling with fragmented memories, and while she forgot quite a lot, including her name, she had still inherited her past life’s dream of succeeding as a singer, dancer, and entertainer! Now going by the name of Hestia, follow this rising star as she throws this new world into a musical revolution as its first dragon idol!

The story has been heavily edited due to all the feedback and critique I’ve received over my 3 years of writing the story, hopefully making the story flow better in its current book form. I personally believe so, as I do have over 3 years of world building finalized and practice writing prose, compared to when I started the web series.

To those who read the story on Royal Roads, Scribblehub, or my Patreon, this means I’ve changed some scenes slightly, or majorly, while maintaining the overall plot of the story. I can assure you, this will be a new experience!

My story is being published by Aethon and they were the ones responsible for the fancy cover I got for the book—ah, our silly little dragon looks so confused. In addition, they also had Erin Bateman narrate the audio version. She’s one of the narrators for Isekai Magus. Considering the story is about our MC bringing her music to this new world, you should give the audio version a try!

Here’s the synopsis!

>A new world. A new primal form. Survival of the fittest reigns supreme!  
>  
>If waking up in a new world isn't bad enough, Hestia starts her new life as a small, newborn dragon in a hostile forest filled with vicious monsters. With status screens obscuring her vision, her first task is to escape two hungry kobolds wanting some grilled lizard for breakfast.  
>  
>Equipped with fragmented memories from her past life and the game-like mechanics of her new reality, Hestia must face trials, beasts, bosses and more as she grows in size and power.  
>  
>She is determined to find civilization. For nothing will stop her from fulfilling her one true desire: to become an Idol.  
>  
>Don't miss the adventure of a lifetime in this new LitRPG Series about an underdog rising up to be the idol her new world didn't know it needed. Hestia may start small, but one day her power will match her determination.
