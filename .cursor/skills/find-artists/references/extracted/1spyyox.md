## Post body

Hi Everyone!

It's been about 6 months since I started releasing the Last Dainv. I've been writing this story since 2016, but due to work, didn't bother releasing it then.

Since we are now at 6 months, the current RR release is at book 4! (Books 1-2 was really accelerated release).

If you like stakes where emotional impact takes precedence over world ending danger and the mc always has to save the world *every week*, then this might be for you.

**So what is it about?**

The story's MC is Gale who will continue to dig deep into his humanity as his abilities put him at odds with his own sanity. Each battle is more about who will stay after they see who he truly is, and most say he's a monster.

*^("Caring for people isn't all-in or nothing, Gale. It's not like flipping a switch. You can't turn on and off whether you care about someone. Even when you think you've shut everyone out, some light still gets through the cracks." — Rachel to Gale)*

The world is bleak and grim. Everything is trying to kill you and shatter your sanity. **Yet the main cast still choose to care about each other in spite of it.**
