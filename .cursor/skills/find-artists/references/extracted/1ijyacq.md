Not being \*complete\* assholes, they will send everyone on Earth to a dumping ground of a planet, where other races have been sent. This time is no exception.

A kind alien sends Zed and a few others to the planet ahead of time to prepare the way for humanity. This is his story, as one of the forerunners.

This is the second version of the audiobook, with new narrators: Adam Stubbs and Mia Fothergill. I love what they did with the book and am happy that they took it on. I decided to be hands-off this time around and let them have creative freedom to do what they wanted with the text, and I'm glad I did. The only change I had them make was in the first sentence to fix the pronunciation of "Iowa" (I think it was ee-uh-wuh-- they're British).

I hope you enjoy listening to it as much as I did.

If you got the first version of the audiobook and Audible won't give you this one, let me know and I will do my best to hook you up with a free promo code. While supplies last, and all that. Thanks, everyone!
