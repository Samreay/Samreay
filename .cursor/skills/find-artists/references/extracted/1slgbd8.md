[**Path of the Devourer**](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0GSSF8KC8)

The apocalypse is here. By the end of the month, 99% of humanity will be slaughtered. But not Ethan Cross.

Deemed a Champion by the mysterious System, he loses all access to traditional stats and skills—the powers that allow for everyone else on Earth to survive. Instead, he will gain the magic of whatever monster he eats.

And it just so happens that the System has brought in a *lot* of powerful monsters.


But despite his potential, the life of a Champion is not easy. The message ‘**SLAY THE CHAMPION TO INHERIT THE CLASS**’ is spread throughout the entire continent, spawning a ruthless manhunt from monsters and humans alike. To survive, Ethan will have to take on hordes of undead, bloodthirsty hunters, and city-razing Griffins—and that’s just in the first weeks.

Still, Ethan finds himself thriving in this new chaotic world. He gets stronger with every enemy he defeats, discovering synergies within his monstrous transformations to propel them far past their original limits.

And though his allies will be few, they are not to be disregarded. After all, nobody wants to fuck with the guy who’s got a dragon on his side.
