Greetings, citizens of Reddit!

I'm back with book 2 of **Elydes - Tides of Change**. I still can’t believe the success of book 1. I’m not great with social media, but I wanted to thank you all for the amazing support. When I published my first chapter on RR to say I had given writing a try, I never expected to make it a full-time job (currently on book 4). 

I’m so excited to share the final edited version of Elydes - Tides of Change out on KU! I’ve spent months editing the manuscript from the version from RR. I can’t wait to hear what y’all think.

A thousand thanks to Francel Garrotte for the cover!

**Blurb:**

Kai has returned to the estate to continue his apprenticeship. With the past debts settled and his family safe in Greenside, he’s determined to learn spellcraft and find his path on Elydes under his teachers' demanding guidance.

But not everything can be taught in a secluded bay. The Guide rewards challenge and strife. Kai must travel outside of familiar places and meet people who might not have his best interests at heart.

In muddy alleys and gilded palaces, powerful forces are contending for the future of the Baquaire Archipelago. Voices whisper of dissent. Kai wishes nothing more than to mind his own business, but laying low has never been among his talents.
