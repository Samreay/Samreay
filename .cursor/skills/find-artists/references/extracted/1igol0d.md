The audiobook version of Book 3 of Nameless Sovereign is out on Audible! It is once again narrated by Griffin Puatu, the same person who did books 1 and 2, and he has done an amazing job once again. If you've been waiting for the audiobook to release to catch up on the series, this is your chance!



Here's the blurb for the book:



Red has never been closer to the power of a true cultivator.



As the inheritance trial nears its end, the challenges only grow deadlier. The competition is fierce, and Red must rely on his hidden powers to outsmart opponents far stronger than him in order to advance.



Winning the trial promises not only a path to power, but also answers to burning questions. Who was the cultivator behind the inheritance ground? How does the moon’s curse affect Red? And what is the nature of the red mist inhabiting his body?



Outside the trial loom greater dangers. The coming beast horde will force former enemies into a tentative alliance, but even that may not be enough. A demonic presence has infested the town, and the boundary between this world and the Infernal Realm grows thin.



To survive, Red must master every skill at his disposal… no matter how forbidden.
