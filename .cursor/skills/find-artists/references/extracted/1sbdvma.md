## Post body

Hey all,

Launching a new series, Conquest of Eternal Seas. It's xianxia with a grindy, satisfying pace, emphasizing the feel of slow progression and achievement. It also has a major character focus, without harem or any of that nonsense. No disposable jade beauties who fade into the background, fatties who get left behind when the protagonist jumps past them -- instead, I wanted the Sect to feel alive and full of characters who are legitimately following their own journey as cultivators.

[https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/160691/conquest-of-eternal-seas-fishingcultivation](https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/160691/conquest-of-eternal-seas-fishingcultivation)

(there are, however, arrogant young masters with slappable faces. because some cows are sacred for a reason)

It also has... fishing! Wanna see the protagonist use a magical lure that he remotely controls to outwit, outfight, and outplay a variety of magical spirit fish? I hope you do, because that's what I've written.
