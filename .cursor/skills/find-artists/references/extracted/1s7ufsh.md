Let's get the synopsis out of the way:

**I thought transmigrators were supposed to start with a cheat skill after getting isekai’d  - so why do I get a countdown timer instead?!**

I was enjoying the NEET life, filling most of my days with gaming, anime, mangas and novels.

One day, some asshole on an internet forum convinced me to try reading books in real life. Little did I know that this decision would end up costing me more than I could imagine.

**\[Soul Contamination\] - Your soul shall be corroded in 1047 days.**

**What to expect:**
**⚔ Weak to Strong (also Tactical) MC.**
**🌤 Grim Fantasy**
🖽 An **RPG-lite system**
🗺 A **living, breathing , deep, intricate** **world**

Two weeks ago, I posted another thread here about this story gaining some traction.
Two weeks later, I basically doubled my followers, and lots of new people are actually enjoying it!

Huge thanks to the 10-20 guys who checked it out back then, especially the ones who decided to stick through the slow start.

As of right now, I'm more than satisfied with its current growth - even if it slows down, I can see it reaching 500 followers next month.

This already means I can commit to my original plan:

* **1,000-1,400 chapters** for the full thing.
* Around 3m-3.5m words, currently sitting at 1/10th of that written.
* **No stubbing** of any kind, be that Amazon or any other type of contract.
* There will also be **no advanced chapters** (beside the free bonus chapters) in **Vol1**, which will likely be around 170 chapters, and the same thing might hold true to Vol2.
* I might open a Patreon later, but honestly, this has been a passion project from the start, and some beer money from there is worth less to me than splitting the readership.

All in all, I'm extremely grateful to those who decided to give it a try, and would be happy if more of you checked it out on Royal Road:
