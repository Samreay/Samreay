Hey everyone!

It’s me, Luke, here again to bring you my latest work that I have no doubt you’ve all been waiting for – Starbreaker: Volume 5! Available for both your reading and listening pleasure this one picks up right where we left off in Volume 4 and keeps the relentless pace we’ve built up! Even more exciting, I am also happy to announce that we’ve locked in the date for Volume 6, the *last* book of the series, for August 4^(th)! So consider snagging it now so you don’t miss it when the book launches!

Of course, it goes without saying that if you somehow haven’t given this series a shot – now is the time! With 5 books and well over half a million words to read, (or sixty hours to listen to!) this series is guaranteed to keep your intrusive thoughts at bay for a while!
