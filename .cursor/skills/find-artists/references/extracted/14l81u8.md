## Post body

Hello everyone. Book 2 of Tree of Aeons is out on KU today. It's the story of a kingdom building magical Tree that's a bit of a paranoid dude that gradually becomes more trusting as he gains more power and feels more secure, while trying to perform what is called a table-flip on the way things are.  

You can find the book here on Amazon KU (so it's FREE if you have a KU subscription)- [https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0BZ168P68](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0BZ168P68)

No audible, sadly, as it's delayed till mid September. 

Please help me out, leave a rating, a review, or just add it to your library (if you have KU). Thanks so much.
