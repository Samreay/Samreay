Hi everyone,

This [EpicLit.net](http://EpicLit.net) update brings you book lists and an updated home page. Lists are the perfect way to group a subset of books. Here are some notes about this update:

\- Lists can be public (visible to everyone), private (visible only to you), or shared (visible to anyone with the shared link).

\- You can browse lists, and search them by name. You can also search for lists that contain specific book(s).

\- When visiting a list page, you have access to all the "Browse" filters (release, genre, edition, and account filters). You will also see the number of books you've read in that list.

\- Patrons can clone lists and see their full stats.

\- Lists can't have more than 1,000 books.



There is still some list-related work for me to do (e.g., adding a liked lists page to user profiles).

As always, I thank everyone who provided feedback. I'd like to especially thank u/Tumble-Bumble-Weed (owner of https://progressionfantasy.co.uk/) for the early testing; their suggestions and bug reports made this update better!

Thanks.
