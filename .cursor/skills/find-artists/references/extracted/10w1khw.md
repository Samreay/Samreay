## Post body

DSidd, your resident monster taming author here to let everyone know I’ve just launched the Kickstarter for the sequel to Beast Mage! I hope you’ll give it a look-see.

[https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/dsidd/a-monster-taming-fantasy-novel-and-audiobook-storm-totem?ref=8q7tlf](https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/dsidd/a-monster-taming-fantasy-novel-and-audiobook-storm-totem?ref=8q7tlf)

STORM TOTEM is available as a signed paperback, audiobook (narrated by Travis Baldree), or ebook. You can also get bundles with Beast Mage or my other books included as well. All paperbacks come signed!

You can check out some of the inspirations for Mana Beasts in the graphic below. I like to call it “Digimon meets Avatar: The Last Airbender”. It's progression fantasy featuring elemental-based magic and evolving beast companions.

https://preview.redd.it/3gj3vkhunrga1.png?width=400&format=png&auto=webp&s=eb46584817842c470533755b6e7b1760cba9d50f

Here’s the spoiler-free description (since it is a sequel):

*Kellen and his Mana Beast, Vex, have taken their first steps to greater power. Unfortunately, in Oras, there’s no rest for the weak.*

*Across the land, the power of the great totems is failing. Fanatical cultists are on the warpath for the first time in a thousand years, spreading chaos on a crusade of vengeance.*

*Seeking answers and a place to hone their mastery of mana, Kellen and Vex face their greatest trial yet: the Storm Totem.*

*Danger lurks at every step inside the colossal structure. Climbing higher and higher in search of a sacred artifact, they’ll discover forgotten lore, powerful new abilities, and even deadlier foes.*

*A reckoning is at hand. A storm is coming. And Kellen and Vex are right in its path.*
