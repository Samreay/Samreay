Dungeon Monsters Surge!

Normal people hid from the monsters. It was the smart play when dungeons overflowed and flooded the forests and plains with bloodthirsty creatures. All Daniel ever wanted was to be one of the brave few who put their lives on the line to cull the dungeons and kill the monsters.

Corruption in the Kingdom had him running for the southern lands and coming face to face with the monster races he had only heard about in stories. Goblins, trolls, kobolds, and ogres, all fighting against the forces of evil.

Daniel finds himself in the right place at the right time and receives a quest from a kobold cleric. Now bound to a dragon, he must become strong enough to protect his charge and save the world from a possible horrible future.

With the minor blessing of a god, and a desire to see the best in people, Daniel just needs to find some people he can trust and a little magic to ensure his place as an Adventurer.

**A slightly cozy Adventure Fantasy following a Paladin, a Dragon, and their Adventuring Party.**
