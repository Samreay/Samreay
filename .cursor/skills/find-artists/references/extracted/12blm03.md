## Post body

Hello everyone!

I'm Phil Tucker, author of:

* [The Immortal Great Souls](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0BZT6D741), an epic progression fantasy set in a hell where heroes are endlessly reborn into a millennia-old struggle to conquer the Pit.
* [Dawn of the Void series](https://www.amazon.com/Dawn-Void-Book-Apocalypse-Trilogy-ebook/dp/B0BTJBV1H9), a completed LitRPG apocalypse where Earth is invaded by horrific demons who test humanity's resolve and force a homeless man to rise as a hero.
* [The Chronicles of the Black Gate](https://www.amazon.com/Path-Flames-Chronicles-Black-Gate-ebook/dp/B01DTTO9MI?ref_=ast_author_dp), the original epic fantasy quintet whose success allowed me to write full time starting way back in 2016.

I'm here today to celebrate the launch of The Rascor Plains, book 2 in The Immortal Great Souls series, but also answer any questions about Dawn of the Void (which launched in Feb), or anything else really.

https://preview.redd.it/08r17qowqvra1.png?width=800&format=png&auto=webp&s=e2cf2704c0abab6fa29ff2d6974d9796313a26c4

A couple of quick things before we kick this off:

* [Bastion](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B09KNXZZR5) is currently Kindle's Monthly Deal, which means a 30% discount.
* The Rascor Plains audiobook will drop this Sept and will be narrated by Nick Podehl; the paperback will drop within a week or two.
* The Rascor Plains launch has been marred by terrible copy editing; I've parted ways with my copy editor, and am actively working on fixing the manuscript as quickly as possible. I'm deeply apologetic, and this won't happen again.
* The Dawn of the Void trilogy will be completely published this year, including audiobooks.
* Next in the pipeline will be my Krieg Chess trilogy, which is a SF PF featuring gladiatorial team chess-inspired combat in the far future, and then Skadi's Saga, my Norse-inspired PF.
