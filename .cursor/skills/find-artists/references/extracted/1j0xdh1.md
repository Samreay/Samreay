### Comment mfeyx6z (parent t3_1j0xdh1)

[Master of Bones](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0DYTQHJWH) is the first book of my new series: God of Undeath

Set in the same world as my [Dark Ascension series](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CTFCVCYT) but 300 years later...

(You don't need to read Dark Ascension to read this...but it'll probably be a little more fun if you do - my goal is to write a whole bunch of prog fantasy series' all in one epic, shared universe.)

**The pitch**: Aurion, a young, reluctant necromancer, is a Soul Eater, a unique individual capable of absorbing the souls of the dead and stealing their power. He was born and raised by a secretive order of necromancers to destroy the Autarch, an immortal tyrant-king, as well as to devour the gods themselves, thus restoring balance to the universe...

And then a whole bunch of crazy shit happens - including an alien invasion - and things start to go horribly wrong...


**Blurb**

**AURION WILL BECOME A GOD, ONE SOUL AT A TIME**

Aurion Asar is a necromancer.

Born and raised amongst the Withered Isles, he has been specifically crafted by a secret order of death magicians to be a soul-eater: an individual capable of eating the souls of the dead and absorbing their power.

Autarch Marak rules the world with an iron-fist. He is cruel, godlike, and immortal. Under his tyrannical rule, hundreds of millions live in constant fear, driven to work ceaselessly so that the Autarch can realize his boundless ambitions.

It is Aurion's destiny to destroy the Autarch, liberate the empire, and unchain the citizenry.

But destiny can be denied, and not all stories have a happy ending.

Accompanied by Justinia, a former assassin, acolyte, mercenary, thief, and generally insane killer, as well as Stumbles the Undead Cat, Aurion will first have to discover the limits of his own power and decide just how far he's willing to go to defeat the Autarch.
