Hi all! I'm excited to say that the next installment of Victor's adventures is available for the Kindle and Audible audiences. Before I blather on, here are the links:

[Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Titan-Blood-LitRPG-Progression-Fantasy-ebook/dp/B0CJ99R853)

[Audible](https://www.audible.com/pd/Titan-Blood-A-LitRPG-Progression-Fantasy-Audiobook/B0CJ5WQDNL)

Do you like berserkers? Do you like quick and brutal fights? Do you like an MC who says what needs to be said and does what needs to be done? Maybe you're not sure, but you know you like progression and/or LitRPG. In that case, give it a try!

**\*\*If you've ever found yourself clutching pearls or feeling faint due to crass language, this series may not be for you! Expletives abound!\*\***

If you've been on the fence about this series, now's a great time to give it a try. There are now four good-sized books on Kindle and Audible, two more on Royal Road, and I'm currently working on Book 7.

In this installment, Victor gets a glimpse at the wider universe, explores parts of a new world, meets new friends, and, of course, makes some new enemies. Victor's a character who's grown a great deal, and you start to really see that in *Titan Blood*, both with regard to power advancement and personality. Victor gets put through the wringer, but he's a tough hombre with some pissed-off ancestors, and he's usually up to the challenge. I hope you'll check it out :)

And now he seems to be right back at the bottom of the barrel.
