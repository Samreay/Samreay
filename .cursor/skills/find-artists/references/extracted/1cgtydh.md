I always imagined what it would be like to write a regressor or returner story where the MC wasn't the only one who retains memories of the past. This is my take on the premise!

[Link to Amazon KU.](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0D2ZJYGD8)

\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~

**Blurb:**

Rieren Vallorne, newly ascended goddess, accomplishes two divine acts before she dies: stealing the gods' system so that everyone can access it, and ensuring she’ll return to her past to restart the whole apocalypse.

Unfortunately, when Rieren goes back in time, she isn't the only one who remembers the previous timeline. Everyone else does too.

Even worse, the gods' meddle with her regression. By the time she reawakens, the end of the world is already underway. Her sect has been invaded, an imperial decree of mandatory cooperation lets her old enemies do whatever they want, and all her old allies are too embroiled in their own troubles to help.

In the midst of the chaos, Rieren comes to the only logical conclusion: she’ll have to reach and surpass her original heights. Fast. Good thing she retains the fully-evolved, divine class from her past.

Rieren can restart her life with one small but important step in godhood already.

\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~\~

There are no romance or harem elements. There is also unfortunately no audiobook just yet, but I have plans in the works :)

Edit: Forgot to mention, the art is done by the amazing Kerim Aykuz!
