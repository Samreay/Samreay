## Post body

Hey there! I'm excited to say that book 3 of my Pokemon-inspired series is out today in ALL formats and on Kindle Unlimited. It's called **Makanuele Rumble**, and it's about the gang competing in a huge Jekua tournament. In addition to tons of battles with new, weird Jekuas, there are also plenty of slice-of-life moments with your favorite characters and perhaps some new ones.

[Illustration by Baj Singh \/ Design by Deranged Doctor Design](https://preview.redd.it/4nin0tpiqpna1.jpg?width=1800&format=pjpg&auto=webp&s=91273a49092889c31eefb52a07d5f62379948bf2)

Here's the synopsis:

>Summoners travel from all over the world to compete in the biggest Jekua tournament in the Volukho Isles: the Makanuele Rumble.  
>  
>Alani's sacred pilgrimage brings her group to bustling Makanuele at this crucial time. Her next stop is at the heart of the city, deep within an ancient crater. The Church led her along this path, but as the journey grows more difficult, she questions whether it is a path she wants to walk at all.  
>  
>Meanwhile, Balt and Niona have been training for weeks just to earn a spot on the tournament bracket, but the competition is fierce. Facing savage Jekuas and harrowing environments, every battle may bring them closer to the champion's title – or else bring their journey to a premature end.  
>  
>Yet as the group struggles with their inner demons and the mounting pressures of the tournament, greater dangers are prowling in Makanuele's streets.

It's available in ebook, paperback, audiobook, and on Kindle Unlimited today! Yes, **all** formats!

The audiobook is dual narrated, with the male POV chapters handled by Graham Halstead and the female POV chapters by Rebecca Woods. They do an incredible job bringing this enormous cast of characters to life.

Here's the [US Amazon link](https://www.amazon.com/Makanuele-Rumble-Jekua-Book-3-ebook/dp/B0BT8F7KBQ/) and the [UK Amazon link](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Makanuele-Rumble-Jekua-Book-3-ebook/dp/B0BT8F7KBQ/).

Plus, books 1+2 are 99c/99p in the US and UK so it's easy to catch up if you haven't started the series yet!

Also, like the first two books, this one includes 3 bestiary entries illustrated by the incredible Tom Parker. Here's his illustration of the weird, mysterious Xamaneki. 

[Illustration by Tom Parker](https://preview.redd.it/xaps4vncqpna1.png?width=3543&format=png&auto=webp&s=dee83163a758618cefec838454e120afc1e55c9a)

With the release of this book, we're officially halfway through the series, with book 4 scheduled for September. Thanks so much for checking the books out. I'll be around all day to answer any questions or just chat!
