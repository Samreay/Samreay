Good day to you!

To celebrate a full week of sharing my book and release of chapter 8, I'd like to introduce my story!

I'm JT, nice to meet you. Just launched my first book **9 April**. It would mean the world to me if you would "give it a shot". I hear rumors that those who read chapter three feel compelled to hit "next chapter". Just a rumor, though!

**"Okay buddy, what's it about?"**

*Pro-gamer child-god reincarnates you into a hardcore aRPG*.

**"Huh?"**

Okay, okay. Here's the blurb:

**Healing: Forbidden.**

**Potions: Scarce.**

**System: Disabled.**

**Assistance: None.**

These are the rules of **Ruthless**, the difficulty setting of the parallel world where a dead soul finds himself being sent for his second chance at life. Unaware that he is part of a sadistic experiment, gifted Union Army soldier **Ludik "Snow" Ruby** must solve the secret behind his recurring nightmares and survive in the front lines of war as he slowly uncovers the true reason there is no healing magic in Jorden.

Snow and his team, the legendary **Special Assaults Group Expedition** (SAGEs), will experience the grim realities of war, forge powerful bonds, encounter mythical creatures, and discover the truth behind 8,000 years of bloodshed.

Will he persevere in an unforgiving world of magic and wonder, against all odds?

Will he survive in Ruthless?

**What to expect:**

\- Fast-paced action, nerve-wracking stakes

\- Swordplay and magic, increasingly powerful and ridiculous in scope

\- Honorable, yet pragmatic MC. Every victory has a price

\- Tragedies of warfare, War is hell

\- No harem

\- 2,000-3,000 words per chapter

\- 3 Chapters a week, 2 Action, 1 Lore appendix

\- No one is safe. It's Ruthless after all

Sounds alright? Why're you still here?!
