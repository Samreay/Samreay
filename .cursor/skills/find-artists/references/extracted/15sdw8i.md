Since I was drawing close to a 100k words (the first book is heading towards the climatic arc) and I also got the right cover artist to do a rendition of The Fool card, I figured that this might be a good time to promote the story.

You can read it on [Royal Road](https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/60890/the-legendary-fool-deckbuilder-litrpg) for free!

The Legendary Fool by Daoist Enigma

Synopsis:

Thomas Lowe had always believed that he was destined for a life of mediocrity after a decade of working a dead-end job in rural America. But fate had other, greater plans for him.

A single card changed his destiny. Whisking him away to the mystical land of Artezia, Thomas gained access to power beyond his wildest imaginations. A power that came at a price--- for now, every Noble House and Ancient Clan in a world where \[Soul Cards\] could be used to summon the elements and beckon lightning from the skies to smite one's foes, would seek to take it away from him at the price of his life.

The name of that card was...

\[The Fool\]

\[Rank: Legendary\]
