## Post body

Hi everyone, I wanted to share my series Emilia Transmigrated. I see a lot of requests here for stories where the progression feels earned and follows a logical path, rather than the MC just stumbling into a secret power that makes them a god in three chapters, so I figured this might fit.

The story starts right at birth. The protagonist, Emilia, is reborn into a world with a "Great System," but she starts at absolute zero. One of the main things I tried to do differently is making the growth resource-locked. You can't just meditate your way to the top in this world. Power is tied to physical resources like specific minerals and mana-herbs, which forces Emilia to actually interact with the world and take risks to get what she needs to advance her mana pathways.

Instead of just "leveling up" through combat, she focuses heavily on the synergy between skills like Herbalism, Talisman crafting, and Formations. The progression comes from her understanding of these systems and how she combines them to survive against much stronger threats. The first book is a grind for survival and basic competency, and by Book 2, the scale expands into ancient ruins and more complex arcane arts, but the growth remains grounded in the rules I established early on.

If you like cunning protagonists who have to outthink their problems because they are physically vulnerable, you might enjoy this. It’s a slow-burn, immersive world where the payoffs feel significant because of the work put in.

Books 1 and 2 are both out now.
