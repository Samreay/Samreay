Hello good people! I'm here to introduce you to **Mimic & Me!**

eBook: [https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CCJ78V21](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CCJ78V21)  
Audio: https://www.audible.com/pd/Mimic-Me-Audiobook/B0CDCLSH6G

.

There is a lot to say, but I'll try to keep it light. We can start with the MC. He's an ordinary, down-on-his-luck adventurer trying to make ends meet. He's a scout, a trapper, and a dungeon navigator...someone from the masses. The Mimic...is nothing like him. Eternally hungry for human flesh, cookies, sammiches, and Cake. All-you-can-eat-kind-of-cake. Shenanigans ensue as the two try to steer what has become their new life in a direction that won't get them killed or eat any more people. Alive ones at least. 

**The cover art** is by Linggar Brammarty, who has also done most of my other artworks.  
**📘 eBook/Paperback:** The book is now available on Amazon in Kindle, Print, and Kindle Unlimited.

  
**🎧 Audiobook:** The audio is out as well and comes in at 17 hours. It has been produced by Soundbooth Theater, which is in all honesty like a dream come true. Ryan and Dorrie are fantastic, but having Jeff playing the mimic... (ELLC anyone?)

**Blurb:**  
**Damon has become a monster...sort of.**  
  
Betrayed and left for dead by a group of slaving nobles, he thought there was nothing left but to bleed out as he counted the number of bricks on the ceiling. But fate had other plans.  
  
Moments before Damon's death, a gelatinous, gluttonous mimic reveals itself to him. Fearing a more gruesome and horrible death down the mimic’s gullet, Damon offers the food in his pack—sandwiches, cookies, and everything else! Encouraged by the (admittedly forced) generosity, the Mimic fuses with Damon, enhancing his body and transforming him into a half-man half-mimic monstrosity.  
  
Once a poor, down-on-his-luck scout, Damon now has new capabilities far beyond anything anyone on Basania has seen before.  
  
**The only problem? His new bodymate’s insatiable hunger for cake...and human flesh.**
