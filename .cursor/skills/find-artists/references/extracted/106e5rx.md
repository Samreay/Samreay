Hi r/Progressionfantasy!

I'm thrilled to announce that Modern Patriarch Book 1 has finally reached completion at a total of 120,000 words across over 400 pages! All it took was eight months, a few sleepless nights, and my growth as a writer.

This is a slice-of-life cultivation story that is centered around a powerful cultivator choosing to use his powers for the betterment of the world instead of perpetuating the endless cycle of violence; a cycle that has been plaguing the world of Eliria for centuries.

It is a story of human emotions, about understanding, about forgiving, while remaining true to the Xianxia spirit--- for there are artifacts to be unlocked, races to be discovered and martial techniques to be mastered. There are interesting, detailed power systems to test, Esoteric Daos to uncover, ancient mysteries to be unearthed and... if I told you everything, wouldn't that spoil the fun?

Best of all, you can experience the entire story as it was originally meant to be experienced.
