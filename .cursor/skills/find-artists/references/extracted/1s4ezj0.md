Its exactly as the title says, midway through writing this book shit hit the fan, I got sick, and a lot of stuff crumbled as I was traveling around with family problems and romance problems too (hence the break up). My artist lives in a foreign country and had problems there, so I needed to get a quick replacement from photos I had previously to make a diy cover. Had to push back the publish date once and struggled through writing every day, but now the book is complete and I hope you guys would like pick it up!


The book is a regression opmc which focuses less on just aura farming(although it still has plenty of that), with more world building and character dynamics in there. If that sounds like something you'd like to read, check it out: https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0GQK2SQ65


Here is the plot:


Sol Lance spent decades as his clan's greatest warrior, the strongest in the Southern Continent. He committed atrocities in their name, wiped out millions, and solidified himself in the peak of power, becoming revered as the greatest warrior to ever live.


He dies wallowing in regret.


He wanted to travel to the Northern Continent, but his father never allowed him. He wanted to use his powers for good, not for slaughtering other clans. He wanted to grow up, adventure, discover the world's secrets, and eventually marry and settle.


Then he wakes up. Twenty again - but still keeping his powers from his old life.


This time, he won't let anybody control him. He will murder those who wronged him and sail to the Northern Continent, where he will seek out adventure and do things himself. He'll learn the skills he wants, see the places he wants to see, and ally with whom he pleases. And if anyone tries to drag him back, he'll make sure they never speak again.
