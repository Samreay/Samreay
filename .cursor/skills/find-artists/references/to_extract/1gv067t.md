All, I’m excited to announce the third and final installment of the Arcane Cultivator series now in audio narrated by Neil Hellegers!



To celebrate, the first ebook is FREE and the second and third ebooks are .99 cents in the US and UK, so whispersync and get the whole set!

  


Arcane Cultivator blends LitRPG, deckbuilding, and cultivation into a story about a sect of desert-dwelling water harvesters forced by the gods to compete in a brutal tournament that takes place every one hundred years. It has elements of found family, training montages, supernatural occurrences, and a system that increases the pressure on the days leading up to the tournament.



The third cover features ‘Elder Brother’ drawn by my wife, Sor, (who has done all of the covers in the Arcane Cultivator series).
