Being that I mainly exist within the anime & manga fandom hemispheres, Shonen has been what I would typically label a story like mine. However, since being on this subreddit, I have learned of a whole new world & community of readers who enjoy this fun way of storytelling.  
  
I am the author of a recently released series called Millennium Exile.  
It is a Progression Fantasy/ Shonen story, that is being written as a series of Light Novels / Novellas (depending on your classification).  
  
Book 1 was released last June, with an Audiobook adaptation having released a few weeks ago – and with Book 2 scheduled for release later this year.  
While volume one is a little light on the world count, please just consider this an introductory book.

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_  
  
Have you ever felt alone? Shunned? or misunderstood?  
Well, that’s what Vincent felt growing up in the streets of the Ryuuga Ghettoes.  
Born with super-human strength, his unusual power saw him branded as a monster and ostracized… despite his good intentions.  
Nevertheless, Vincent imagines being a hero of justice.  
  
Attempting to realize this dream; he uses his incredible strength to help those in need, regardless if they want his help or not.  
  
One day, he is confronted by a mysterious man who offers him a chance at a new life…and to know what true strength is. Through this encounter, Vincent is inducted into the ranks of GRAVE; a clandestine anti-terrorist organization- thrusting him into war and a world he never knew existed.  
  
It isn’t long before a mysterious entity from a forgotten age, is awakened and enters Vincent’s body; bestowing him with strange new powers that are both a gift and a curse.  
Vincent must not only come to terms with his new and deadly abilities; but also the long-hidden truth of his past – along with the real reason the people feared him.  
Follow Vincent on his journey, as he travels across the mysterious region of Kou – an area rich with lore-filled locations, magical creatures, and untold secrets of an alien invasion.  
  
Armed with a power that had been exiled for a thousand years, will Vincent be the key to the world's salvation or its demise?
