I bring you The Bladeweaver.

A hero with swordspells. A decapitated blood mage. The world isn’t ready. Neither are they. 

Kale never asked to be summoned into a dungeon. He definitely didn’t expect to find a severed head just lying around. Or a dagger once wielded by a goddess. Now, with ancient magic in his hands and Liliana (the blood mage head) as his opinionated companion, he’s caught in a conflict far bigger than either of them.

Some rave reviews from early readers:

"I didn't read it." - My sister

"I was promised swords and magic. This book contains both." - A very satisfied, possibly real person

If you like swordplay, blood magic, and a hero who actually has emotions, with a mix of serious stakes and witty banter, this might be for you. Also, I’m personally a big fan of the cover. Shoutout to the legendary Yona Saura for that masterpiece.
