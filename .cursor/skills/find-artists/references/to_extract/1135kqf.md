Hi r/progressionfantasy, 

Some of you may know me from the other stories I write (Modern Patriarch and The Legendary Fool). This is the final draft/iteration of an older story I started a year ago, but couldn't finish because life and other stuff coming in the way.

I was preparing this book for its ebook launch, but it felt wrong to publish without giving the royal road readers a chance to read the story to completion. 

Cultivation in general is a theme I'm quite fond of and also one I've delved into across more than a few hours, while LITRPG is a genre that crystalizes excitement in numerical form like no other. Blending the two has been more fun to write than I thought, so if any of this looks up your alley feel free to give it a shot! 

Pun intended. 

(If there was one highlight of writing this book to completion, it was the half a dozen martial arts I managed to tack in there, haha).

Born for the Apocalypse Book 1: Void Marked

Note: The current cover is a placeholder till my commission comes through. 

Synopsis: Jack Anderson hadn't quite been expecting his lazy sunday afternoon to be rudely interrupted by an alien incursion, but he supposed it was one of those things you just don't see coming.

Now that it was here though, well, every man had to confront their destiny one day. 

Doomsday Prepper meets the Apocalypse.

Chaos ensues.

What to expect:

- Martial Arts 

- High octane, heart thumping action with tension aplenty. 

- Realistic side characters

- Gritty survival 

- Interesting skills! Well designed loot! New classes on the horizon!

- Dao and Cultivation based abilities.
