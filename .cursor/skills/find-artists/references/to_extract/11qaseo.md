My second book Scions of Sylum is over on [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0BXT33JBG) as of today. It’s a pretty classic/standard sort of LitRPG book, and if you’re thinking about checking it out, here are some of its bigger selling points.

* There is a ton of content, more so than the vast, vast majority of LitRPG books out there. According to Amazon, the first book is 781 pages, and the second book is 936. For comparison, the first book of the Cradle series is 291 pages, so reading these two books is about the same amount of content as a full six Cradle books. If you’re looking for a longer read, this one is for you!
* Despite having remained on the site for over a year, the books maintained a 5-star grammar rating on RR, and that was before I edited the books for Amazon. Even if you’re a hell-bent grammarian, the writing should hopefully feel very clean and polished.
* I’m a little biased but I think it is a nice and cool book :)

If you haven’t read book one, I also made an earlier post about it here.

For the blurb,

>***The threat of dark gods has passed. The forest is safe. The deadlands have stopped expanding.***  
>  
>Now, all Tess really wants is a vacation. Unfortunately for her, fate has other plans.  
>  
>Quickly discovering that she's in far more danger than she ever knew, Tess will have to train, level up, and protect herself at all costs. So when an unexpected benefactor shows up with an unlikely solution to her problems, how can she refuse?  
>  
>Follow Tess as she battles monsters, explores new cities, delves into dungeons, and builds up her settlement, all while meeting a strange new cast of characters along the way.  
>  
>***Scions of Sylum*** **is a classic LitRPG novel with character levels, classes, and stats, ideal for existing fans of the genre and newcomers alike.**
