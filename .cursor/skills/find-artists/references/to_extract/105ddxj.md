### Comment j3c3sqc (parent t1_j3ajo8l)

The progression here primarily works through training. Early on Gabriel gets himself a wristcomp with a stat tracker that shows him how he's doing on improving his core attributes (like strength), his skills (like ranged combat), and later disciplines which are like perks.

There is also the progression of equipment. Starting out from a scrub in fatigues and a dream to one who actually has weapons and armor.

More importantly it's the rediscovery of magic in a world that has long forgotten about it. You could even say its existence has been suppressed. And that adds on more of a cultivation bend to the progression.
