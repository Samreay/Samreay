Tree of Aeons is the tale of a dude getting reincarnated as a magical tree in a world with heroes and demons. Matt, our protagonist, will look after a village, watch it grow, defend it from demons and other folks, grow, and more!

It's a story inspired by turn-based strategy and civilization building games, and those familiar with 4X games will find a lot of the elements familiar.
