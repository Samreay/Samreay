Read [Reborn on a Systemless Earth... With a System - Vol. 1](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0C38ZXWZC) on Amazon and Kindle Unlimited today.

It's a hilarious, queer postive, reverse-isekai LitRPG that parodies typical Progression Fantasy tropes by throwing them into some of the most ridiculous scenarios you'll ever see in one of these books. There's a strong focus on really good characters, and the card-game-based RPG system will constantly give you that gacha fix. Our hero starts out as a lowly D-Rank \[Adventurer\] but rapidly climbs the ranks towards S-Rank supremacy.

Blurb:

>*Gain ALL the levels. Earn ALL the Destiny Cards. Destroy ALL of California.*   
>  
>You know the setup: A young man on Earth hit by a speeding truck who ends up in a generic fantasy world.   
>  
>But what about this: A young man in a generic LitRPG fantasy world hit by a carriage who ends up in modern-day San Francisco, and yet still wants to be a hero anyway? Join Eryk Solbourne and his two Earthen friends Francis and Delta as they adventure across the United States and try to achieve the impossible: becoming the ultimate hero in a world where levels don't even exist.

Featuring the following:

Card-based RPG mechanics being abused and min-maxed.

* Underground fighting circuits.
* Karen.
* Slow-burn, queer-positive character building.
* Vicious satire of Isekai and LitRPG tropes.
* Bear wrestling.
* Multiversal temporal desychronization causing cosmic chaos.
* Bizarre numbers of references to CATS (dir. Hooper, 2019).
* Perpetually annoyed lesbian wives.
* Plot twists so massive you'll be dropping your damn jaw to the floor.
* ...And more!

[Please enjoy Systemless](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0C38ZXWZC) and share it with your friends and rivals.

Cover art by [Mikayla Buan](https://www.instagram.com/mikayla_buan/). She's so great at these covers!
