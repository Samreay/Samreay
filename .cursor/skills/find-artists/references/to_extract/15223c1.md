**Hello** /r/ProgressionFantasy, I am Strungbound, and I'm happy to announce that Book 2 of Ghost of the Truthseeker is now available on Amazon and Kindle Unlimited.\*\*

Book 1 was a success and I learned a lot from my experiences in becoming a first-time author and used that to help in writing Book 2. One thing that I think Book 1 helped me prepare for in Book 2 was characters, like really getting their voice to be what I wanted. I don’t think I necessarily was atrocious with voices in the first book, but I think in the sequel I ended up more satisfied with characterization and giving each character their unique voice and traits.

A little bit about me, I’ve been a huge fantasy fan my entire life. Before Ghost of the Truthseeker, I had given writing a go a couple times casually, but this story is really where I’ve poured all my energy into.

Ghost of the Truthseeker is a story that combines cultivation and LitRPG elements. I was inspired by Defiance of the Fall in its sprawling and lived-in world and style of progression for my story elements, as well as Cradle, and more oblique drawings from Tower of God and Omniscient Reader’s Viewpoint. Also, I am just a fan of xianxia in general, more people need to check out some pure xianxia stories.
