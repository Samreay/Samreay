## Post body

Street-orphan Lina steals a dingus from a couple of black-robed wizards, and gets instantly dragged to a necromancy school. I’m writing grounded progression fantasy with slow burn power accumulation, no System, and a tough-as-nails female main character.

The theme of the first book is salvaging a life from trauma, surrounded by death and undeath. Lina doesn’t trust easily, and the story doesn’t pretend that’s simple to fix, but the mood is still pretty light and funny. The worst that could have happened already happened, now we’re getting over it.

I’m on Royal Road Rising Stars now, so now’s a good time to check it out!
