## Post body

Howdy all!

Runebound book 4 just released but I'm currently in the ghoulish land known as Australia and it's the middle of the night, so I want to sleep. I'll keep things brief. 

Runebound follows a music teacher, Noah, from earth who dies and gets reincarnated in the body of yet another teacher. Unfortunately, this particular teacher was a major scumbag and is universally hated. He's also meant to teach magic, which is a bit of a problem as Noah has absolutely no idea how magic works yet... but he's got a few tricks up his sleeve to find out.

[Check it out here!](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0D45W82CD?ref_=dbs_m_mng_rwt_calw_tkin_3&storeType=ebooks&qid=1724245133&sr=8-5)

[Cover by the very talented Kaion, through Aethon!](https://preview.redd.it/443bym3qo0kd1.png?width=885&format=png&auto=webp&s=7fbab62fdc0bb7b5df053f333856aba9e9595a82)

The novel is a mixture of light xianxia inspiration , a unique rune based magic system, a whole lot of bluffing, mystery, and a dash of mature romance. (Mature in that the characters are mature about things, not in that I'm competing with Fourth Wing to see how many cheeks can get clapped in the span of one book).

Okay. Now I'm going to bed. Please read my book so I can get dopamine from seeing the happy number go up. Cheers and goodnight/goodmorning to the civilized portions of the world.
