Here's my synopsis:

**Defy destiny. Ascend beyond the realm of gods.**

For thousands of years, the cycle of fate repeats itself: the Death God is reborn, only to be slain at the hands of a \[Hero\] chosen by the Goddess of Life. Knowing his death is inevitable, Nox the Death God instead meddles with his reincarnation so that unlike his predecessors who came before him, he would retain his memories, his death magic abilities, and his vast pool of mana in his next life.

Now reborn in the body of Eli— a young orphan in the small city of Velmond on the outskirts of the Sanctus Empire— he sets off with a plan to break this cycle of fate once and for all. And the first step to achieving his goal is to become a \[Healer\] and enroll into the Holy Academy of the Church of Life.

Having already reached the pinnacle of power once before, Nox will do it all again. But this time, he will become even *stronger.*
