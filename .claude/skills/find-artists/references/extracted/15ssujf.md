## Post body

Hey y'all! Very excited to share today the cover for the next book in my Pokemon-inspired Jekua series, **Whispers from the Poisoned Isle**. 

https://preview.redd.it/obdwncr0phib1.jpg?width=1800&format=pjpg&auto=webp&s=32aec8c61855efdc96bef66c26346bdb0f50ee29

As always, the illustration is by [Baj Singh](https://www.artstation.com/bajsingh) with design by [Deranged Doctor Design](http://derangeddoctordesign.com/). You can head to the post over at [FanFiAddict](https://fanfiaddict.com/cover-reveal-whispers-from-the-poisoned-isle-jekua-4-by-travis-m-riddle/) to see the full paperback spread, which I love. 

The book will be out **September 19** in ebook, audiobook, paperback, and on Kindle Unlimited. You can preorder the ebook and audiobook now, either on [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Whispers-Poisoned-Isle-Jekua-Book-ebook/dp/B0CDZGB7XZ) or [Audible](https://www.audible.com/pd/B0CFRNCTSD). 

Below is the blurb. Thanks for checking it out! 

>*Yuluhu Island was once a thriving ecosystem, but now it’s a toxic wasteland.*  
>  
>*After barely surviving the attack on the Makanuele Rumble, Balt and Alani set out with their friends on a dangerous journey across the island to find a laboratory that might hold the key to learning what truly happened to Balt’s grandparents eight years ago.*  
>  
>*The Summoners will have to battle mutant Jekuas warped and strengthened by their toxic environment, search for a way to repair the mana leak that threatens to plunge Alani into perpetual soulsickness, and avoid the attention of their enemies.*  
>  
>*But surviving the island won’t be enough, because the supposedly abandoned lab is still in use – and a threat deadlier than any they’ve faced before is waiting.*
