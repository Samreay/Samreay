Two of my books have come out in the last month, and I think that they'd be a good fit for... like, a small percentage of the people on here? It's some weird niche stuff! But I think they're pretty cool, and the romance in them *will* make your teeth rot, and the girls are cute!

**Heart of Dorkness!**

Heart of Dorkness is a YA fantasy, very found-family focused, with some very cute gays being extremely clueless. They can barely lock eyes without blushing. It's good for people that want a simple, sweet adventure, with endearing characters, neat worldbuilding, a respectable number of dragons, and a romantically-clueless lead who eventually figures it out! Two volumes covering a couple of years, but the story is complete!

Read if:

You like cute stuff

You like sweet more than spice

[Cover by SlothBeing!](https://preview.redd.it/x7oe26hatmxg1.jpg?width=1500&format=pjpg&auto=webp&s=b349078d0055e7a507846d2c39c1cbec21c6dc6a)

Ivil Antagonist is more adult. It's a space opera featuring a particularly strong lead who is looking for love. She finds it, and then really doesn't know what to do with it, since her modus operandi for most problems is to blow it up, and that's not a great foundation for romance. It's a pretty long rom-com, self-contained in one big volume!

Read if:

You like endearing big dommy mommy

You like spicy lewds... in space
