## Post body

Hey Progression Fantasy Subreddit!

I'm excited to share my story: The Sword in the System.

The Sword in the System is an Arthurian Time Travel Isekai. Which means, a character is flung into the distant past to experience Arthurian Era Britannia! The catch is... It's alternate history! Not to mention the addition of a System...

Check it out if you enjoy:

* A main character starting from the bottom and working his way up! (of course)
* LitRPG and progression (there are stats and levels, woo!)
* Hard earned experience and fights!
* Characters that have more dimensions than a piece of paper!
* Unique (and not so unique) cast of enemies, creatures, and adversaries

Here's the blurb:

>**Pulling the sword from the stone didn’t make him a king. It sent him back to save one.**

>Eben was a lawyer. He hated it, but that’s what his ex-wife wanted. 

>When his father-in-law fired him from the firm, Eben had to get away.  He booked a hiking escape to the Scottish highlands. Seeking shelter from a sudden storm, he ducked into a cave and found a sword lodged in stone.

>How could he resist?

>He grabbed the sword. But instead of becoming King, it sent him into the distant past.

>Emerging in medieval times, a system gives him a mission:

>Prevent Camelot’s fall and King Arthur’s death before the world ends—taking him with it.

>Monsters stalk the lands, born from the minds of men.  Camelot lies beyond a veil of mist, guarded by deadly fae, and villages burn in the wake of a mysterious warlord.

>Without any clues to go on, he’ll need to figure out how to save a world he’s a stranger to. 

>He spent his life being what others wanted. Now, ripped from his own timeline, he'll discover who he is and what he stands for.
