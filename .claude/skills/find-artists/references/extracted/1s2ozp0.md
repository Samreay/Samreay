## Post body

[Slay Hero Gilgamesh](https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/145195/slay-hero-gilgamesh-golemancer-villain-litrpg)

&nbsp;

**Premise:**

An untalented leper of a powerful Magi clan holds the name of the prophesied Hero, but that name was not meant for him. Before his clan could sacrifice him in a ritual to give that name to its rightful heir, the whole world is pulled into a series of brutal Trials for the right to enter the realm of the gods. The origin of all myths.

Gilgamesh narrowly escaped certain death, but the ritual continues. He is weak and doomed, while his rivals are blessed with power and destiny he could only dream of. Even so, Gilgamesh is resolved to do whatever it takes to prove himself worthy of his name.

The path to greatness is long and full of thorns, but no one is more willing to walk it than a leper with a stolen name.

&nbsp;

**Selling points:**

* Smart protagonist. He's not a genius but he's calculating and learns quickly from whatever mistakes he makes to perform better next time.

* A genuine villain. Not an anti-hero that sometimes does bad things and feels bad about them. Not a fake villain who just acts the part. He is a true villain by choice as that is his only path to survive and achieve his ambition.

* But he is not a psychopath that loves killing and causing misery. He doesn't commit evil for fun. He's just willing to do whatever is necessary in a world that doesn't want him to win. MC has a goal and will not compromise on it.

* Tons of formidable rivals. Everything from having god tier abilities to supergeniuses to being literally chosen by fate. Many competent enemies too.

* No talent, cheats, or excessive luck. Gilgamesh only has his cunning, ruthlessness and sheer unrelenting ambition to contend with them. He doesn't whine or break down. He endures and takes calculated risks to gain power by any means as he claws his way to the top.

* No plot armor. Stuff happens independent of him that sometimes works in his favor, but only because he takes advantage of the opportunity. The universe will never bend over backwards to save him from a stupid decision.

* Huge, HUGE character development. I like writing characters more than anything else (aside from worldbuilding when I should be writing). There's a lot of personalities throughout the series and they'll all dynamic.

* Fleshed out golemcrafting system. MC is a golemancer and he'll stay a golemancer for the whole series.

&nbsp;

Give it a try if you like villainous, competent, and strong-willed protagonists.
