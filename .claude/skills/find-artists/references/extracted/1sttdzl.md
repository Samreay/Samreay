## Post body

Hey everyone, I finished vol.1 of a Progression Fantasy Story and finally launched it online!

The title of the story is: The Oathbound Chronicles: Heir to a Mad Mage

All readers and followers are welcome 🤗. I had a blast writing it, and can’t wait to see everyone in the pages!🙌

Blurb:

The Ring chooses. The weak don’t survive….

Sami thought the voices were in his head.
The girl. The monster. The whispers. He was wrong.

A quiet librarian with terrible luck, Sami’s life unravels when his visions lead him into the woods—where the girl is real, the monster is waiting, and death is seconds away.

Lyra saves him. The voice doesn’t

Accept the Ring?

Unlock Magic?

Now he’s bound to an ancient Ring with a deadly past, and a legacy that has already claimed its previous heirs.

In a world where power decides everything, Sami has no choice:

Grow stronger.

Or become the next failure.
