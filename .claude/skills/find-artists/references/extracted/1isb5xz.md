I’m thrilled to announce that Oath of the Survivor, Book 1 is now available!  A huge thank you to Aethon Books who helped get the series ready for publication!  You can find it on Amazon via print, Kindle/KU, or Audible, narrated by Adam Verner.

Kyle’s story begins where Earth’s story ends.  When calamity strikes the world Kyle Mayhew called home, he is thrust into a struggle for survival.  Alongside his Central Health Autonomous Diagnostic Drone (C.H.A.D.D.), he will have to overcome mutated creatures, ruthless marauders, and his grandfather’s legacy to carve out his place among the stars.

If you’re interested in reading a story with a weak-to-strong MC, quippy companions, and a long-spanning story, please consider checking it out!
