Tyler Smith wanted to kill a girl but fate had other plans.

 Offered the opportunity for redemption, Tyler finds himself escaping death in one world to only face it in another. Rescued by the Princess Alina, a fearsome knight and nothing like a princess at all, Tyler finds himself with no choice but to join her as she investigates strange happenings in the land.

 Together, they will discover traitors, rescue their allies, defend themselves from forces that wanted them dead and they will discover a truth that the world never knew. A truth that will change everything.

 A truth that will help Tyler become the hero he never could be on Earth.

 **WHAT TO EXPECT**

 • A large supporting cast

• Slower character moments and world-building

• A story about the characters, the relationships, the world and the evolution within

• Political intrigue, Empire wars & Fantasy races

• Graphic violence and the odd expletive

• A system design (inspired by WoW) but it won't be the primary focus

• There may be romance but no wish fulfilment. MC will need to earn it!

 **THIS DOES NOT CONTAIN**

 • Harem

 **SCHEDULE**:

 • Min 3 chapters a week, Sun - Fri @ 11pm GMT (6pm EST)
