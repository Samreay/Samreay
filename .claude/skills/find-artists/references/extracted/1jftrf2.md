Hello everyone!

Author of the Ashborn Primordial and Magnus series here.

Super excited to announce the launch of my new LitRPG Isekai Progression Fantasy on Amazon! This story is the product of several minds and a ton of passion, and is basically my love letter to Isekai and LitRPG. With this series, I really wanted to hit all the tropes we know and love about the genre, while putting my own spin on things. It's got weak to strong progression, my usual worldbuilding, unique combat-crafting powers, berserking elves, whimsical gods and dragons... All the familiar stuff, but packed in fresh ways.

Or that's  the hope, at least. This story has all the learnings from Ashborn Primordial and Magnus (and I've had several past readers tell me this is their new fav fic from me), but you'll have to tell me how I did!

At 188k words / \~680 pages, there's plenty  to sink your teeth into!

The audio is 20 hours long, and narrated by Christian J. Gilliland, who did an awesome job bringing the characters and world to life.

Synopsis!

**What do you do when the god who summons you won’t say why?**

Greg walked into a bar hoping to wash away the sour taste of a bad day at work. What he got instead was a bartender offering him the opportunity of his dreams—with a catch. A ticket to purchase a class that could make him the strongest being alive. *If* he can afford the asking price.

Thrust into a world he knows nothing about, Greg will need power on another level entirely. To get ahead, he'll need the kind of power only deities possess...

***Good thing the whole world’s your playground when you can make anything level up.***
