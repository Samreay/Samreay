## Post body

Hey, everyone! 

I’m incredibly excited to finally be able to share our Kickstarter campaign for the hardcover version of Sufficiently Advanced Magic, the first book of Arcane Ascension. I’m working closely with Wraithmarked Creative — the awesome folks behind the hardcovers for books like Mother of Learning, which was one of Sufficiently Advanced Magic’s inspirations — and Podium Audio, my awesome audio book publisher. We’re already hard at work on editing, artwork (by Daniel Kamarudin, the original artist), layout and design (by Shawn T. King, who did the design for the paperbacks for AA2-4 and W&W).

Gotta love the trouble coat and our second-favorite magic sword. (Dawn is first, of course. We can’t let her get upset.)

  In addition to the book itself, the Kickstarter campaign will also include the option of getting a premium bookmark, similar to the ones Wraithmarked has created for previous campaigns. You can see a preview of that below.

https://preview.redd.it/9uxwjkvqvwka1.png?width=700&format=png&auto=webp&s=03aadfd9a483c430fe3cd89e6d853e7851d8e5f3

The campaign [is live now](https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/wxp/sufficiently-advanced-magic) and will run for roughly a month, so if you’re excited to get your hands on a premium-quality hardcover, please go take a look!  You can find it here: [https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/wxp/sufficiently-advanced-magic](https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/wxp/sufficiently-advanced-magic)
