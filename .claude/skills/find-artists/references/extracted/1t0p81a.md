## Post body

I just launched my second story on Royal road earlier this week!

It's a cultivation story, because I love cultivation as a power mechanic. They're usually pretty boiler plate, though. I wanted to spice things up. So not only is this story set in a solarpunk-inspired, Polynesian-influenced, European-based feudal aristocracy, which is rare in and of itself for a cultivation story, the cultivation system is fully planned out with ten stages, and it includes everyone unlocking a unique plant-based power of their own.

As for the story itself, I was inspired by characters like Battle Beast in Invincible or Kenpachi in Bleach, characters who love combat. I wanted an MC who is intense and battle crazed in the heat of the moment but generally pretty chill. The MC spent a very long time with a single-minded focus on the pursuit of his purpose, which is to die a glorious death in battle, and as a result he's kinda clueless about what it is to have a life. And now life is thrust upon him when he as to start life over in a new universe.

Consider checking it out!

Blurb:

After over a thousand years of constant cultivation and battles, the first 10th stage cultivator in recorded history tore through the fabric of the multiverse and challenged the gods themselves. He lost, but they refused to grant him the glorious death he had longed for his entire life.

Now, he has to start over as Silas Eru, a weak fifteen-year-old royal heir in a new universe, a universe with a completely different cultivation system. To make matters worse, for the first time in his long life, he has to deal with having actual friends and family… and deal with being a part of the aristocracy.

Through it all, Silas will continue on his path, and he *will* have his glorious death!
