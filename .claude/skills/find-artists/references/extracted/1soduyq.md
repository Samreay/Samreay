## Post body

I just finished the first book of my series Chasing Nightmares - Breaking Point!

[https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/155252/chasing-nightmares-post-apocalypse-litrpg-psychological](https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/155252/chasing-nightmares-post-apocalypse-litrpg-psychological)

The first volume covers the beginning of her career as a Hunter, the revival and rise of a cult that wants to reshape society, and Alex coming to terms with the trauma of her childhood.

It's only 74,000 words, but I feel really proud to have finished it.



*"An unresolved past rots through the present and taints the future.*

December 31st, 2055. The day of humanity's first contact with the incomprehensible cosmic entities in the wider universe, causing mass upheaval around the world.It's been one hundred years since then, the apocalypse came and went, but humanity is still stuck repeating its past mistakes.

The Archmagus Lodge asks the universe questions, uncaring for who answers.

Second Adventists want to bring salvation to the world, under the boot of their messiah.

And the governments of the world are now mere shadows of their former selves, clinging to any power they have left.

Alexandra Summers was born into this world in the theocratic dictatorship that took over former Canada. Now an escapee and recent graduate of the monster slaying Hunter's Guild, she's finally gained access to the guide network and the upgrades it provides. Her dream is to become a named hunter like her hero Artemis and be strong enough to never be at the mercy of others again. 

The past never stays buried though and she's burned more than a few bridges to get where she is. Now she'll have to reap what she's sown while old enemies bring back past traumas and new tortures. All while fighting the eldritch horrors that haunt the world. 

What to expect"

\- Traumatized FMC pushes past the breakdown to finish the mission

\- LITRPG power ups are smaller or more utility based.

\- Horror

\- No romance (characters may flirt, but that's it)

\- Queer characters 

\- Mix of Modern and Medieval weapons with magic

Influenced by S.T.A.L.K.E.R., SCP, The Witcher series, Wasteland series, and Bleed Out by the Mountain Goats

UPDATES: Mon / Wed / Fri

This is a blanket content warning.⚠️

There will be dark topics covered though very rarely directly and those chapters will have additional warnings.
