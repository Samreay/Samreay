Book 6 in the Journey of Black and Red story is out! [Here](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0C376LNYZ?notRedirectToSDP=1&ref_=dbs_mng_calw_5&storeType=ebooks) It will cover the Mexican war and a certain trip to the Mediterranean to retrieve a certain sword and also eat the locals.

If you’ve never heard of Journey of Black and Red, it’s a not-litRPG and not-cultivation vampire progression fantasy that follows Ariane as she samples her way through the dark corners of the world. I have prepared a small FAQ below.

**What’s the blurb?**

Ariane wakes up in a cell, confused and lost. She is a prisoner to a group of rude people affected by a curse. And there is this strange thirst water cannot sate. In the shadows on 19th century America, there is more to fear in the night than mortals. For example, her.

**What’s the genre?**

Technically Gaslamp fantasy. The setting is mostly historical with supernatural elements. It’s not a tragedy but I’ll be the first to warn that the beginning can be brutal.

**Is there romance?**

This is not a romance story though there are minor romance elements later on.

**Is it really prog fantasy?**

Yes, the setting has easily identifiable threshold of powers. The MC leanrs soft skills, like knife throwing, the spear, and guns. The protagonist progresses and the level of power of various characters remains consistent.

**Trigger warnings.**

Journey has gore, violence, slurs, mentions of sexual violence, racism, sexism, gaslighting, and torture. The main character is a vampire with the associated low empathy. If something triggers you, you should probably not read it.

**Where do I start?**

Well if you’re curious, it’s also free on RR! Here is the link: [A Journey of Black and Red | Royal Road](https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/26675/a-journey-of-black-and-red)

That way you can decide if it’s for you or not. To reiterate, the beginning is the character’s lowest point so expect some pain.
