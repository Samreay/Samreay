## Post body

The Petralist is an action-packed YA epic fantasy with lots of humor and a unique magic system using rocks. The system is revealed over time as the characters discover truths concealed for centuries.

**Book One is Set in Stone**

**Impossible choices. Explosive magic. Brilliant humor.**

Connor is cursed . . .

And in Obrion, that means he must obtain patronage from a high lord prior to his sixteenth birthday, or face execution.

Connor turns sixteen tomorrow.

Should be a simple thing until his small town is invaded. Armies clash, superhuman Petralists step out of legend, and layers of deadly intrigue shatter Connor's simple life.

If he stays, he dies.

If he leaves, people he loves will die.

Some birthdays are like that.

And there's this girl . . .

Set in Stone launches Connor and his friends into an epic adventure filled with humor, unique magic, and enough twists and turns to braid bacon into a noose.

Other main characters include:

Hamish - Connor's best friend. His habit of sucking on rocks holds the key to an ancient power not seen in generations.

Jean - the local healer. She has no magic, but is the smartest person anyone knows.

Verena - one of the invaders. Her bag of tricks can stop entire armies.

The Petralist series is complete at 7 main titles, 3 prequel novellas, and 1 book of short stories.

(cover art by Brad Fraunfelter)
