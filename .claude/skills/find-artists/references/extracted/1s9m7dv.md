## Post body

Book 3 of Nightmare Realm Summoner just dropped today! For anyone who hasn't checked the book out but might be interested, the series is about a burnt-out college student who is having the time of his life surviving the apocalypse.

He has a summoning-focused class in which he gets his monsters powers after they die and until they come back to life, so expect a mixture of summoner mixed with raidboss style combat. This is a -- to put in non trademarked terms, LITRPG Apocalypse story, so expect a few of the what I hope to be the fun tropes that tend to go with those.

[Check it out here!](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0G3QR8JFP?ref_=dbs_m_mng_rwt_calw_tkin_2&storeType=ebooks)

And check out the cover!

[By Admira!](https://preview.redd.it/ach9hfe79lsg1.png?width=1003&format=png&auto=webp&s=b41589cb81a54b32a632e7f96e582996f1f3dfc4)

I really love how this one turned out. Super excited for this book, it was a very fun one to write and I hope it's equally as enjoyable to read.

I've also rolled a... less than ideal time to launch. But I'm starting to realize that a 'less than ideal time' tends to be basically every time something comes out nowadays. Amazon is completely deep-fried right now, and if you want proof, take a glance at the latest Primal Hunter book, which is sitting with 50 reviews after many days of being TOP 50 on the entire platform. Yeah... uh, reviews are fine guys. Nothing to worry about.

So here's to hoping that this is the least ideal it gets and things get better from here! Until then, whether it's me or any other author you happen to like, now more than ever -- please consider dropping a review on their book to help support their launches. Given that a series as big as Primal Hunter has only gotten 50, then you can imagine how much every single review matters now.

Audible coming shortly, I've been told by my publisher that it'll be up on Audible in 1-2 months. Unavoidable life delays happened. But shouldn't be too much longer!

For those of you wondering about Rise of the Living Forge 5, it is in the same boat. Roughly 1-2 months. Thanks for your patience\~

Cheers all!
