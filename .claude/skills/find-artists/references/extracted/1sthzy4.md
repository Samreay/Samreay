Hey everyone!

Book 2 of the Ethersmith has just been released! It's a runesmithing and dungeon hunting series, and this entry focuses heavily on the former with a crafting tournament arc and other fun (and not so fun) shenanigans. Book 1 had an awesome launch. Thanks to everyone who has supported making this dream come true!

Book 2 Blurb:

**Vivi survived the demon prison—barely.**

With her body wrecked and her bounty still active, she and Lucius retreat into the one place no sane ether hunter would follow: the Crimson Blight.

Surviving it will take everything she has. But the Blight is only the beginning. Beyond it lies Shivenar, the city of runesmiths, where she’ll need to prove herself to a world that still never takes her seriously.

Her runeswords are unlike anything Shivenar has seen, and the wrong people are starting to notice. That's problem enough on its own. But she has a dangerous secret, one she barely understands herself: her potent void core.

**No power comes without cost.**
