Hey folks, I'm Nameless Author, and I'm back to announce the release of book 4 of Nameless Sovereign.



This is a story inspired by years of reading cultivation novels such as Reverend Insanity, Renegade Immortal, and Lord of Mysteries, and wanting to put my own spin on it. I'd like too thank everyone that has read and stuck with me through the releases of the first three books as well as the series in general. If you have enjoyed the first three books, give this one a read, and if you have not read it yet and it seems like something you'd enjoy, please do take a look.



In this fourth book, we see the arrival of the beast horde and the appearance of a mysterious yet vaguely familiar threat in the middle of it. Here, Red and his sect find themselves at a crossroads.

Here's the blurb for book 4:



Necromancers, demons, and ghosts—steeped in the forbidden arts, Red risks losing himself to the darkness he seeks to control.



His efforts to protect his companions have come at a price and he finds himself entangled in a dangerous game with forces beyond his comprehension. Possessed companions, relentless Curse Breakers, and a sinister head lurking in the shadows – the trials have only just begun.



As the beast horde descends, the Water Dragon Sect will be tested like never before. There’s more going on than meets the eye, and failure will have dire consequences.



Even should they succeed… things may never be the same again.
