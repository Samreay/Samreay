## Post body

Hey everyone,

**Book 4** of [The Lone Wanderer](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0FTZZRDF3) just dropped on [Kindle](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0GKCW6P2S) and [Audible](https://www.amazon.com/Legend-Third-Hero-LitRPG-Adventure/dp/B0GX79CT34) (well, there is currently a technical issue so you can only get the audiobook through the amazon page, though it will hopefully be resolved soon).

This is a **worldhopping LitRPG** about a young mage who awakens a bizarre ability: he can send fragments of himself to random worlds across the universe. If they survive, they return with new spells, rare treasures, and powerful secrets.

The story jumps between those off-world adventures - like navigating a toxic gas planet while inhabiting the body of a floating cat-like creature, fighting in a coliseum illuminated by a crimson sun that never sets, or cutting through a jungle of bloodthirsty vines - and Percy’s life back home, where he has to take the time to fit his gains into his versatile and growing arsenal, thus turning them into real power.

Naturally, there are those who covet Percy's secrets, stopping at nothing to get their greedy little paws on them and prevent him from reaching the pinnacle of magic.

This release is a bigger milestone than usual, since Book 4 wraps up the entire **Winter Saga**, bringing together plotlines that have been building since Book 1 into a major finale. If you’ve been waiting to start, this is the perfect time to binge Books 1–4. And there’s more on the way - Book 5 is in final edits, Book 6 is nearly done, and the story is far from over.

Huge thanks to **Austin Rising** (narration), **Sergio Chaves** (cover art), **Mountaindale Press** (production and publishing), and all of you for the support!

Enjoy :)
