The fourth book in my Cardsmith series has just been released on Amazon and Kindle Unlimited:

[https://www.amazon.com/en/dp/B0FBN8C3YQ](https://www.amazon.com/en/dp/B0FBN8C3YQ)

Irwin has burned monsters, braved portals, and pushed his card forging forward at a speed unheard of. While Daubutim fights a battle of minds in the Library, Irwin prepares for the next step in his growth.

With dangerous beings hunting cardsmiths growing ever more active, he slots his sixth card. With chaos everywhere, he must forge his heartcard.

But forging a heartcard yourself demands more than just skill... it demands a price.

\-----

For those new to the series, it's a weak-to-strong, card-based story featuring forging and music.
