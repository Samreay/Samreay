## Post body

Hey guys,

I recently published my first Progression Fantasy novel on [Royal Road](https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/157835/shatterpoint-progression-litrpg). (10 days ago). It's also a LitRPG. **It's already sitting at 100+ followers after just 10 days!** I'm very happy.

I’ve written four other novels before, but man, I absolutely fell in love with writing this one. I honestly don't know if I'll be able to write anything other than Progression after this rodeo. Book 1 is ready and I'm posting 1 chapter each day. Book 2 is in the works.

Mini blurb:

>When his younger brother falls into a coma, Butch, a retired soldier is pulled into the game-like world inside the boy's mind and must fight through monsters, factions, and broken memories to bring him home.

>The deeper Butch pushes, the harder the world pushes back. Because if he succeeds, the game world ends. Every NPC, every monster, and every blade of grass knows it deep down. And they will do everything in their power to stop him.

Book 1 is finished and edited. I'm into book two now, *but I learned a few days ago that if I don't hit Rising Stars on Royal Road, I'll get buried and receive almost no views. I think I still have a chance, because my stats are good, that's why I'm doing this self-promo kind of thing.*

*Hope some of you will enjoy it!*

*What's different about this book?*

* The game world is fluid and reactive. It's inside the subconscious of a teenager. And MC is trapped inside.
* Progression of MC through stats, spells, and class, but also through mechanical knowledge and exploits.
* Progression of Chicken (MC's sidekick who often steals the show, evolution paths.)
* Die in the game, die in real life
* Sword Class MC **(Think Berserk meets the Witcher.)**

I'd be super thankful if you checked the full blurb and chapters: [**Read on Royal Road by clicking HERE**](https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/157835/shatterpoint-progression-litrpg)**.**

Any comments, feedback on cover/blurb, anything is super helpful, as well.

Best,
Dan
