The story focuses on Isaiah, a former ruler of a galactic empire who upon realizing he is slowly perishing transmigrated into a new universe where magic is a reality, realizing the possibilities magic offers riles up his ambitious nature. However, he soon comes to realize that his talent for mana is beyond lacking. He will have to find alternative paths (formations!) to take as he advances through the ranks and alternative methods utilize mana using his poor mana capacity. He must compensate for his lacking talent through immense resources and his intelligence, to obtain these resources he has to return to his roots before he was a ruler, a businessman.

With 71k+ words (60 chapters) already published, I've crafted a unique and expansive magic system that has cultivation elements to it that will be revealed as the story progresses. Later on, the story will open up to encompass the entire universe.

I recommend reading till chapter 22 ([So Much Fun!](https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/83052/father-of-eternity/chapter/1562311/so-much-fun)) to get a good grasp of the novel to see if you wanna continue reading or not. I hope you check it out!

**What you can expect from this novel:**

**⦿ Ruthless and Calculative MC:** While not without his flaws, Isaiah is an experienced leader and won't make rookie mistakes nor will he hesitate to act for his gains.

**⦿ World Building:** I have some spicy things planned for this part, the story will encompass the entire universe, wait for it to slowly build up during the story.

**⦿ Unique Fighting Style:** Due to his poor mana sense, MC will be primarily using formations to aid in his battles.

**⦿ High Power Ceiling & Distinctive Magic System:** While the build up will take a while, the power ceiling will be really high. Each magic rank will introduce new abstract concepts apart from just raw power increases.

**⦿ Politics & Business:** Mc will have to earn his money somehow right? Well he will return to his roots and become a businessman.

**⦿ Romance:** While there is a romance tag, it won't be present till the later parts of the story, I plan to let it marinate, slowly building it up.

**⦿ Talent & Being OP:** Despite his terrible mana sense, Isaiah's high comprehension talent will allow him to compensate and still be a force to be reckoned with.

**⦿ Consistent Updates:** The novel updates at least once a day, each chapter consisting of minimum 1000 words.
