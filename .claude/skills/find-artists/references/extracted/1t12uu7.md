## Post body

Hello everyone! My series Reader Mage has been ongoing now for 8 months now on Royalroad with 172 chapters out currently, 360k+ words, and more than half a million views. I decided that it was about time I made a promo on here.

If you enjoy urban academy stories with dungeons and towers, and a twin main character who starts out as an underdog and grows both in strength and character, then check it out: https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/129589/reader-mage

Blurb: He read the novel no one else would, and now had a System to show for it. 

Merlin Tyrrell’s favorite web novel, Singularity Mage, came to an end, and with it his dream of getting into one of the most prestigious Mage Academies on Earth.

Evaluated as Deficient—a Mage who could cast no spells—Merlin could only watch as his wish to be able to protect those he loved from the dangers Dungeons and Towers posed was snatched away from his finger tips. That was, until the author of Singularity Mage sent him a gift for being his sole reader until the end: the System of the novel’s protagonist, and his greatest Skill: Reader Mage

Now possessing the ability to analyze and deconstruct spells on the fly, maybe—just maybe—Merlin can finally achieve his goal and end up becoming the greatest Mage to ever exist.

What to expect:  
Twin shenanigans  
Dragons  
Soft magic system inspired by science (mostly biology)

Cover art by: Miru on Vgen!
