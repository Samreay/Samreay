Hey everyone, Mecanimus here. The latest journey is out! For those who know the story, it will cover >!the return from Europe after the knights, up to a certain fae heist of significant impact. !<

For those who don't know Journey, the story tells the tale of a recently turned vampire girl starting in 1803 Georgia and spanning decades of meeting interesting people and then drinking them. The story starts really grim but has its doses of humor. The progression is slow but steady. It is not a LitRPG, there are no harems, and romance exists but it plays a farily modest role throughout the entire story. 

Journey is not on KU due to the exclusivity clause. You can read it in its entirety on RR (although unedited) here: [https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/26675/a-journey-of-black-and-red](https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/26675/a-journey-of-black-and-red)
