Hello ProgressionFantasy,

I am a newish author on Royal Road, and I have a newer series I started called Skyrat. It is a Skypunk LITRPG in which I tried to blend serious action with comedy. It's a fun story and I think many would enjoy it.

Here is the first comment I got which made me laugh: "I swear I will find you and steal three-eighths of your pancreas if ya drop this story out of nowhere. Though seriously, its a crime im the first one here. You're good." Literally my first follower lol.

>*Killer.* 

>*I am a killer.*

>Sentenced to a fate worse than death, Torren was supposed to die on his very first first trip to land. To an island underneath the dreaded mist. His crew had failed him. Lied to him. He was to be nothing more than bait for the **Boss**. Instead, he survived…

>He always survived.

>Thrown from a **Skyship**, Torren should have fallen into the mists of monsters and madness… Instead he landed on the decks of the **Skycutter.**

>Now his fate is claimed by a new captain, and he must navigate the brutal skies of Echo Cores, Islands, and all manners of **Mobs** trying to kill him.

>All for a slim chance at redemption.

>

>**What to expect:**

>\[+\] A LITRPG with elements of Final Fantasy 7 and One Piece

>\[+\] Skypunk aesthetic

>\[+\] Islands that act as Dungeons

>\[+\] GameLIT

>\[+\] A MC with Druid-like abilities

>\[+\] Classes

>\[+\] Stats

>**What not to expect:**

>\[-\] Harem

>\[-\] Romance (probably)

### Comment o75v1zf (parent t1_o74rtid)

Yes, I believe that is accurate enough. Really it's own thing that incorporates elements from traditional steampunk, magitech, pirate lore, and modern references while not betraying it's a LITRPG at the heart. An example of that is the MC finds a pair of Jordan 1's, complete with stats and special effects.

In a way, that's the beauty of it as the islands in the book all have different lore, mobs, and setting.

If you go in expecting very traditional steampunk and/or Sky pirate, then you might be disappointed. If you want something that blends the elements together then I think you'll like it. (and by all means if you read and don't like something, let me know. Always trying to get better)
