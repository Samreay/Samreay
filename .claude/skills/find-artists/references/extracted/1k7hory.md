\*Minor spoilers, but you asked\*

The unlimited respawn is only for those under level ten or less than 100 years old. I wanted to give the MC the ability to have tangible consequences without pulling the rug out of the tension of the story. I explore the social consequences of this, as many choose to remain low-level for security at the cost of being weak.
