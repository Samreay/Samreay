Hello! A first year author here, wanting to share my story to as large of an audience as I possibly can!

I present to you my creation!

**Title:** Unrepentant

**Genre:** Fantasy/Dark Fantasy/Progression Fantasy

**Link:** [https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/88732/unrepentant](https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/88732/unrepentant)

**Cover and Artwork:** Created by a friend of mine called Rose, they prefer to stay anonymous as they are not a commercial artist.

**Blurb:** How far can one man defy convention? When punishment holds no meaning for the condemned, what purpose does it serve?

This gripping tale follows a pair of utterly unrepentant criminals, delving into the depths of distorted morality. Readers will witness the chilling consequences when a man acts solely on his terms. In a blend of Western and Xianxia fiction, this story explores twisted desires and unyielding defiance.

What to expect? A main character who truly only focuses on himself, who will lie, cheat, steal, and much worse to get what he wants. His partner in crime is a magnificent crow spirit beast with an addiction to chaos.

What about the world? A lush continent with several distinct and powerful countries, each with drastically varying cultures and species. Twelve gods reign supreme upon this land, with minor deities worshiped to a lesser degree. However, the last couple of decades have not been kind to the faith of the continent.

Is the protagonist weak or strong from the start? Oh, definitely strong. However, not exactly untouchable—there are certain circumstances regarding this Alchemist’s power…

When will it be released? Will you just disappear if I get hooked on this story and want to read it until the end? It shall follow a release schedule of Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 10:20 PM CEST. This story's first volume is planned out and will be finished. The author is working hard on creating a substantial backlog, with one month of content already prepared and more being made every day. Once a certain amount is reached or the author has more free time, there may be a higher number of releases than currently stated.

I hope you enjoy it, if you give it a chance that is! If not, thank you for reading, I appreciate the time you've given here! :D

### Comment lcy9jl6 (parent t1_lcx8atd)

Well, art is largely subject to ones taste. I can't fault those who like the cover and does who don't. It's a losing man's game to try and find a universally loved image.

It's like the situation of how some artists are diehard supportive of the idea that realism is not 'real art'. Then, you have peeps that believe abstract art is only art if someone convinces you into liking it.

Think Piet Mondrian and his Compositions, one of the greatest artists in history. You either love his work or don't, there is no in between. For example, I can't understand his work, to my eyes, it seems like something a toddler with a ruler and a steady hand could do. Yet, the world recognized him as a highly talented artist based on the work I specifically didn't like. Me not being into it won't change that fact.

And, right now in a sea of covers using ST, Midjourney, DALL-E, etc.

I would rather say, this outlier in style will pull more attention then the current convention which incorporates those generated images. Mind you, I am not saying it is a bad option, whatever you can get when you are starting out is perfectly valid!

However, I do believe, now that AI has become much more mainstream, that a lot of covers have started appearing that follow the same formula. It works, but it is quickly saturating the market, so to say.

Perhaps to illustrate my point, if you take a look at the 'Best Ongoing' section on RR, I would say that two covers stand out the most on the frontpage list: Super Supportive and Soul Guardian. And they are atypical, not conforming to the standard practice for covers on the site.

At this point in time, I would not consider getting another artist to do another version, as this one lines up with what I want the story to have as it's face for now.

I would just be wasting my money, and the artists time if in the end I decided to discard that new work and stick with this one if they can't produce something I would be happy with.

Yet, this again is a subjective thought! Haha, I'll stop myself here as I've gotten a bit side tracked right from the get go. As long as the majority finds the cover interesting/different, that is enough for me! The cover won't keep my readers coming back after all. :)
